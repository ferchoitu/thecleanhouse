How to Install the Rev-A-Shelf RV-35

1

Measure your cabinet

Measure the interior width at the narrowest point. You need at least 11.25 inches of interior clearance. Also measure depth (18.5 inches minimum) and height from cabinet floor to shelf above (17.75 inches minimum).

2

Mark slide positions

Hold the mounting template (included) against the cabinet side wall. The slides mount flush to the cabinet floor — mark the four screw holes on each side with a pencil.

3

Pre-drill and mount slides

Use a 3/32" drill bit to pre-drill the marked holes. Attach the cabinet-side slides using the included #8 × 5/8" screws. Use a level to confirm slides are even — a 1/8" difference will cause the bin to roll.

4

Attach slides to the bin frame

Slide the bin carriage onto the mounted rails. You should hear a click when fully engaged. Pull the unit out to confirm it extends fully and slides smoothly without binding.

5

Install the bin and liner

Drop the plastic bin into the carriage. Insert the liner. Close the cabinet door — confirm clearance on all sides. Adjust slide depth if the door doesn't close flush.

Rev-A-Shelf RV-35 — FAQ

Does the Rev-A-Shelf RV-35 fit a 12-inch cabinet?

Yes — the RV-35 is specifically designed for 12-inch base cabinets. The unit frame is 11.25 inches wide, giving 0.75 inches of clearance on each side in a standard 12-inch interior. Make sure your cabinet interior is at least 11.25 inches at the narrowest point before purchasing.

Can I add soft-close to the RV-35?

Not directly. The RV-35 uses standard ball-bearing slides that don't accept a soft-close add-on. If soft-close is a priority, look at the Rev-A-Shelf 4WCSC series, which uses integrated damper slides from the factory.

Does the RV-35 fit IKEA Sektion cabinets?

The standard RV-35 is not directly compatible with IKEA Sektion. IKEA cabinet interiors are metric-sized and often require an adapter plate. Rev-A-Shelf sells IKEA-compatible versions of some models — check for the "IKEA" designation in the product title before ordering.

What size trash bag fits the RV-35 bin?

The RV-35 uses 35-quart bins, which take standard 8-gallon trash bags. You can also use 10-gallon bags for extra slack. The bin rim is 10" × 15", so most rectangular kitchen bags in the 8–10 gallon range will fit without overhang.
