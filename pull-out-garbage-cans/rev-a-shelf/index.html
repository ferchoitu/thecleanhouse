Rev-A-Shelf Pull Out Garbage Can — All Models Reviewed (2025)

Rev-A-Shelf is the most trusted brand for Rev-A-Shelf pull out garbage cans — and for good reason. Based in Kentucky, they've been manufacturing cabinet organizers since 1978 and dominate the under-cabinet waste container market. Their lineup covers every cabinet width from 12 inches to 21 inches, with soft-close options, double-bin configurations, and IKEA-compatible variants. Here's how every major model compares.

Which Model Do You Need?

If you have a 12-inch base cabinet, the RV-35 is the standard choice. It's top-mount, meaning it hangs from the shelf above — ideal for under-sink installs where plumbing takes up the floor space. It doesn't have soft-close, but at $80–$120 it's the best value in the lineup.

If you have a 15-inch or wider cabinet and want one large bin, the 4WCSC-1550 is the upgrade pick. It's a bottom-mount system with soft-close slides and holds a full 50 qt — enough for a busy family kitchen. Expect to pay $120–$160.

If you want trash and recycling in the same cabinet, go with the 53WC-1835 (18-inch cabinets) or the 4WCTM-15BB2 (15-inch cabinets with soft close). The 4WCTM is the premium choice — it fits a narrower cabinet, closes silently, and holds two 35 qt bins. Budget $140–$200.

The 5349 series is Rev-A-Shelf's budget bottom-mount line. It works for 12-inch cabinets but uses a smaller 27 qt bin and simpler slides. Good if you're on a tight budget or doing a rental renovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Rev-A-Shelf pull out garbage cans made in the USA?

Rev-A-Shelf is an American company headquartered in Jeffersontown, Kentucky. Some pull out waste container components are manufactured domestically, while others are sourced internationally. Assembly and quality control is USA-based. Check the individual product listing for country of origin on specific models.

Where can I buy Rev-A-Shelf pull out garbage cans?

Rev-A-Shelf pull out garbage cans are available on Amazon (usually the best price), Home Depot, Lowe's, and direct from the Rev-A-Shelf website. Amazon typically has the widest selection and fastest Prime shipping across most models.

Are Rev-A-Shelf pull out cans compatible with IKEA cabinets?

Some Rev-A-Shelf models are compatible with IKEA Sektion cabinets using adapter kits. The RV-35 and 4WCSC lines have IKEA-compatible versions. Confirm your cabinet's interior dimensions first — IKEA Sektion cabinets are metric-sized and the interior width may differ from standard US base cabinets by a fraction of an inch.

What is the difference between the RV-35 and the 53WC?

The RV-35 is a single-bin, top-mount (undermount) system designed for 12-inch base cabinets. The 53WC is a double-bin, bottom-mount system for 18-inch cabinets. The 53WC holds significantly more — two 35 qt bins — and is better for families. The RV-35 is the go-to for narrow cabinets and under-sink installs where plumbing takes up the floor.
